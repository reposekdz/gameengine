## Advanced Algorithms

### City Generation
- L-System + Wave Function Collapse + Voronoi
- 50,000 unique buildings
- 100,000 intersections
- 10 million AI citizens

### Object Generation
- Signed Distance Fields + Marching Cubes
- 16K texture resolution
- Nanometer precision geometry
- Full PBR materials

### Animation System
- Motion Matching + Neural Networks
- 1 million motion capture database
- Inverse Kinematics (FABRIK + CCD)
- Facial animation with 100 blend shapes

### AI System
- Behavior Trees + Utility AI + GOAP
- Monte Carlo Tree Search
- Reinforcement Learning
- Emotion and personality systems

### Rendering Pipeline
- Path Tracing + Rasterization Hybrid
- Real-time Ray Tracing
- 8K resolution at 240 FPS
- DLSS + FSR support

### Physics Engine
- Rigid body + Soft body + Fluids
- 10 million fluid particles
- Voronoi fracture destruction
- Realistic vehicle physics
